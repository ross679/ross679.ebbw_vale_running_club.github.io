About

EBBW VALE RUNNING CLUB is a friendly, sociable, easy-going club that was originally formed in 2007.

With a mixture of ages, experience, abilities and interests most runners will find something to suit them at our club.

We participate in events at a local, national and sometimes international locations.

Our members vary widely and include some fast race-winners, those setting out to achieve their own personal challenges and those who are happy with a steady run together with friends.


Sessions

We meet at Ebbw Vale Sports Centre on Tuesday evenings at 6:15

Pricing

The first session is free, we then charge £1 per session, membership is £15 per annum which includes affiliation to Welsh Athletics which entitles you to a discount for any events entered.
